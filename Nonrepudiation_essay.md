
# nonrepudiation_essay

### What is nonrepudiation in the context of digital systems?
- Its purpose is to prevent a user from repudiating actions like sending or receiving data.
### Why is it important for applications like online bookstores?
- It ensures the validity of the user's purchase, preventing them from repudiating it later.
### What other tools/technologies besides logging can help achieve nonrepudiation?
- Digital Signatures  
If the signature is valid, it means the action was done by the user 
- Timestamping Services
This third-party service ensures the time of the action and confirms when the action occurred.
